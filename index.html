About UrbanRide

Born in the streets and raised in the parks, UrbanRide is more than just a skate shop - we're a community. Our decks are crafted by skaters who understand the perfect blend of pop, durability, and style.

Whether you're hitting the local spot or competing in the big leagues, we've got the gear to elevate your ride. Join us in pushing the boundaries of what's possible on four wheels.
